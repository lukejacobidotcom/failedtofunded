Questions before you claim?

Here's what traders usually ask before switching.

What counts as valid proof?

A screenshot or receipt from another futures prop firm showing one of three things: a failed evaluation, a breached/eliminated sim-funded account, or a payout confirmation — with a visible date within the last 90 days and the firm identifiable.

How fast do I get my code?

Most submissions verify automatically in under a minute. A small share get a human review and are answered by email within 24 hours.

Which plans can I use it on?

Any MyFundedFutures plan except Flex. The code is single-use, applies to your first month only, expires 7 days after it's issued, and can't be combined with other promotions.

I got a payout at another firm — why would I switch?

If you're good enough to get paid, you deserve to compare rules, payout speed, and costs. The 70% makes trying us nearly free. That's the whole pitch.

What happens to my screenshot?

Before anything uploads, you black out whatever you want hidden — account numbers, balances, anything private — right in your browser. We receive only that censored copy, re-created on your device with all metadata (location, device info) stripped. The only personal data we collect is your name and email. Redacted images are deleted within 90 days of a decision; we keep a non-reversible fingerprint to prevent duplicate claims.

Can I claim this more than once?

No — one redemption per trader, ever. Codes are personalized and single-use.
