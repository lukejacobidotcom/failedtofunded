Questions before you claim?

Here's what traders usually ask before switching.

What counts as valid proof?

A screenshot or receipt from another futures prop firm showing one of three things: a failed evaluation, a breached/eliminated sim-funded account, or a purchase receipt — with a visible date within the last 90 days and the firm identifiable.

How fast do I get my code?

Most submissions verify automatically in under a minute. A small share get a human review and are answered by email within 24 hours.

Which plans can I use it on?

The code applies to the Builder plan — 70% off your first month. It's single-use, expires 7 days after it's issued, and can't be combined with other promotions.

Will a plain purchase receipt really work?

Yes. A receipt from another futures prop firm proves you've been paying someone else to trade — that's all we need to know. Upload it, black out anything private, and claim your code.

What happens to my screenshot?

Before anything uploads, you black out whatever you want hidden — account numbers, balances, anything private — right in your browser. We receive only that censored copy, re-created on your device with all metadata (location, device info) stripped. We read the firm, date, status, your email, and name off that image — the only personal data we keep is the email we send your code to. Redacted images are deleted within 90 days of a decision; we keep a non-reversible fingerprint to prevent duplicate claims.

Can I claim this more than once?

No — one redemption per trader, ever. Codes are personalized and single-use.
