Questions before you claim?

Here's what traders usually ask before switching.

What counts as valid proof?

A screenshot or receipt from another futures prop firm showing one of three things: a failed evaluation, a breached/eliminated sim-funded account, or a purchase receipt — with a visible date within the last 90 days and the firm identifiable.

How fast do I get my code?

We review entries and email your 70% off code, usually within 24 hours.

Which plans can I use it on?

The code applies to the Builder plan — 70% off your first month. It's single-use, expires 7 days after it's issued, and can't be combined with other promotions.

Will a plain purchase receipt really work?

Yes. A receipt from another futures prop firm proves you've been paying someone else to trade — that's all we need to know. Enter it above and claim your code.

What happens to my screenshot?

Your proof is submitted through our entry form and reviewed by our team to confirm eligibility. Please black out anything you don't want to share — account numbers, balances, or other personal details — before you upload.

Can I claim this more than once?

No — one redemption per trader, ever. Codes are personalized and single-use.
